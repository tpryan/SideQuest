About

SideQuest is a conference about leveling up developer skills, with untraditional developer topics. Think that public speaking could be a good thing to add to your arsenal? Wondering if office politics can help you? Wondering how craft brewing can expand your developer skills? This is the conference for you.

All topics will be told by developers but not directly about development, with a focus on other skills you can harness to drive your development career forward.
