SideQuest is a conference to level up web workers.

Often we make the most progress in our skills, jobs, or careers when we are learning things outside our main focus area. After you've attended enough conferences, read enough books, followed enough tutorials, just learning a new language doesn't do it for you.

That's why there's SideQuest, a conference dedicated to helping you advance yourself in a non obvious way.

  • Perhaps learning public speaking could help your career, so Technical Presentations That Don't Suck could help you.
  • The rise of Responsive Web Design has made SVG a hot topic these days, learning How to Draw SVG Graphics with Illustrator might be a career saver.
  • Perhaps you need some advice in going at it along that you could get from I Started a Video Game Music Studio in Philadelphia Because I Didn't Want to Move could inspire you.

All topics will be spoken by developers but will not necessarily be directly about development, with a focus on other skills you can harness to drive your development career forward.
